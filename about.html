FEATURES OF OUR PRODUCTS

Prickly pear brightens the skin when used to make the lotions, soaps, sprays. These products made with prickly pear promote skin and hair health, improve blood sugar and blood fat levels and promote the liver. Lotions made with prickly pear reduce the wrinkles on the skin. Also, products made with prickly pear help to reduce dark circles and puffiness when a person uses them. The skin becomes brighter and smoother because of how the products improve their skin to be better than before. And they will have the new skin which has no dark spots.

Prickly pear also works on the skin by helping to increase its ability to absorb oils and serum. The fatty acid contained in the oil help to replenish collagen.

Our vision is to be the most influential skincare brand in Africa and beyond as we head towards setting the pace for the skincare beauty industry in Africa. As an aspiring leader in beauty industry, our guiding vision is creation of skin care products that offer original results and quality of life to our customers.

At Prickly Pear Glow, we aim to create and provide healthy and effective skin care products that are designed to treat, rejuvenate and improve skin and enhance our customers looks, as well as improve the overall lifestyles of our customers by helping them look beautiful and regaining their confidence.

We also ensure the complete satisfaction and well-being of our clients, manufacturing high performance cosmetics and promoting innovation, maximum efficiency, competitiveness and social responsibilities. We further aim to be recognised by always trying to reach a compromise between one’s beauty and well-being.

Prickly pear contains high levels of water and this makes it hydrating when applied to the skin. This helps to moisturize dry skin and alleviate symptoms of dehydration. It is also rich in antioxidants, which include vitamin c and flavonoids of which helps to protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, the use of our products can help to prevent premature aging and maintain healthy skin.

Also due to seasonal changes, our products helps to reduce inflammation and soothes irritated skin. This anti-inflammatory compounds are beneficial for calming redness, swelling, and irritation on skin caused by acne or sunburn. The vitamin C content in our products helps improve the overall complexion and reduces the appearance of dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and uneven skin tone.
